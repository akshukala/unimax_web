About UniMax Enterprises


Unimax is a consumer oriented company we are committed to quality service & quality product. Over the past 35 years, our directors & their families have built a solid & strong reputation on high quality products and service. Unimax enterprises deal is all types of hardware like garden hose, grinding and cutting tools, hammers, measuring tapes, screw drivers, pliers, fastners, nuts, bolts, welding rods.
We do planning for other dealers and consumers. Our network of dependable worldwide suppliers guarantee the best quality & service. Adding to this ensure value for money on all our products. Unimax is the ONE STOP solution for any sort of hardware product with unimaginable service & quality. We are been associated with mostly all the leading hardware retail shops, leading promoters and industrialits in and around our base.
